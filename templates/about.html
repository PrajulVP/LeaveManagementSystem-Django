Purpose

Efficiently manage employee leave requests, automate leave processes, and ensure compliance with company policies and labor laws.

Benefits

  • Increased efficiency and time savings.
  • Transparency and trust within the organization.
  • Improved accuracy of leave tracking.
  • Better planning for employee absences.
  • Higher employee satisfaction.
  • Cost savings through streamlined processes.

Features

  • Leave request submission.
  • Leave approval workflow.
  • Leave balances and accruals tracking.
  • Calendar integration.
  • Customizable leave policies.
  • Reporting and analytics.
  • Self-service portals for employees.
  • Mobile accessibility for on-the-go leave management.
