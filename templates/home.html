A Leave Management System is a software solution designed to streamline and automate the process of managing student leave requests within an organization. It serves as a centralized platform where students can submit requests for time off, and teachers can efficiently review, approve, or deny those requests.

Key features and functionalities of a Leave Management System include:

  • Student Self-Service
  • Leave Request Submission
  • Workflow Automation
  • Teacher Approval
  • Compliance and Policy Enforcement
  • Mobile Accessibility

Overall, a Leave Management System simplifies and streamlines the leave administration process, improves transparency and communication between students and teacherers, and helps organizations effectively manage their workforce's time off while ensuring compliance with company policies and regulations.
